Dal-Tadka

Restaurant style dal fry tadka recipes made using dal or lentil is very common across india. there are numerous recipes made with myriad types of lentils, which is made for different purpose and for different occasions. one such simple and easy lentil based recipe is the toor dal or arahar dal based dal tadka recipe known for its flavour from tampering.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

firstly, in a pressure cooker take ½ cup toor dal, 2 tbsp masoor dal. you can also add moong dal if you prefer.

step 2

also add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp salt, 1 tsp oil and 1½ cup water.

step 3

pressure cook for 5 whistles or until dal is cooked well.

step 4

add 1 cup water and adjust consistency. keep aside.

step 5

now in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp ghee and splutter ½ tsp cumin, 1 bay leaf, pinch hing and 2 dried red chilli.

step 6

also add 2 clove garlic, 1 inch ginger, 1 chilli and saute slightly. further add 1 onion and saute until it turns golden brown.

step 7

keeping the flame on low add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp coriander powder and ½ tsp salt. add 1 tomato and saute until it turns soft and mushy.

step 8

add in cooked dal and stir well adjusting consistency as required. simmer for 5 minutes or until flavours are well combined.

step 9

now add 2 tbsp coriander, 1 tsp kasuri methi and ¼ tsp garam masala. mix well.

step 10

finally, enjoy dal tadka with jeera rice or steamed rice.

ingredients

1/2 cup toor tal(rinsed)

2 tbsp masoor dal(rinsed)

Oil or butter as required

1 tablespoon Red CHILLI FLAKES

Salt , as per taste

3 sprig Coriander leaves , finely chopped

1/4 tsp turmeric

1 Onion , finely chopped

2 to 3 Green Chillies , finely chopped

For Tadka

1tsp ghee

1/2 tsp cumin/jeera

pinch hing/asafoetida

1dried red chilli

¼ tsp kashmiri red chilli powder

1 tsp coriander (finely chopped)

Equipments Used

Glass MIxing Bowl

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
